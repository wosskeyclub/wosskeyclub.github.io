Meme of the Week

Why are there memes here? Isn't this a website promoting charity?

Every week, everyone selects a meme to post on this page. Sometimes members aren't participating, and we need a quick and effective solution. Even though it doesn't directly contribute, we post memes to promote participation, which will result in more participation towards our main goals.

A new meme is posted every Monday afternoon!
