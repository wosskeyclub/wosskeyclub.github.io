The Pumpkin Smash was a Smash!

At the end of the day, it was a success. The pumpkin bits all over the grass made it really obvious. Funds were donated to Safetynet.

Thank you to everyone who contributed in meetings or volunteered during the event, and also whoever that brought in items for the bake sale (especially those awesome milkshake cupcakes).
